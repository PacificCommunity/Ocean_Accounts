Pacific Coastlines
Coastlines for Pacific Islands. The data has been derived from OpenStreetMap ways tagged with natural=coastline. Ways are assembled into polygons and then split. Some errors in the OSM data are repaired in the process. More info on OpenStreetMap Land polygons.
